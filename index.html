Subscriptions and Payments

The Quit Smoking app offers optional paid subscriptions.

All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not store, process, or have access to your payment details.

Subscription status and entitlement management are handled using RevenueCat, a third-party service that helps manage in-app subscriptions.

These services may collect and process information as required to provide their functionality. Their data handling practices are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

We do not store personal user data on our servers. Any data processed by third-party services is retained only for as long as necessary to provide their services and comply with legal obligations.

User Rights

Users can:

The Quit Smoking app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
